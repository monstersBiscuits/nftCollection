History

Monster Biscuits is a collection of 10,000 NFTs randomly generated on the Ethereum blockchain, assembled from over 150 unique features and hand-drawn.

The design is based on a biscuit (the BN) to make monsters that could have 10 expressions.

This project aims to fund donations to charities chosen by anyone who owns or has owned an NFT from the collection.
